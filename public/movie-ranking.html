The Best Movies According to Sam

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

This is my favorite Lord of the Rings movie. It has so many cool scenes and is the start to a legendary trilogy and book.

John Wick

I love this movie, I think it's very fun and action packed, and I love the choreography.

Jujutsu Kaisen 0

One of my favorite new anime movies. It's a great prequel to the anime and has a ton of cool fighting sequences, and introduces everyone to one of my favorite characters in Jujutsu Kaisen: Yuta Okkotsu.
